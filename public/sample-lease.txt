═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                  PERMITTED USE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The Premises shall be used solely for the operation of a full-service 
Japanese restaurant (ramen, izakaya-style small plates, and sushi) 
with alcoholic beverage service, including but not limited to:
  - Dine-in service (lunch and dinner)
  - Takeout and delivery (via third-party platforms)
  - Late-night service (kitchen open until 1:00 AM on Fri/Sat)
  - Weekend brunch service with bottomless mimosas

Tenant intends to apply for a Type 47 (On-Sale General — Eating Place) 
liquor license from the California Department of Alcoholic Beverage 
Control (ABC). Tenant estimates that alcoholic beverage sales will 
constitute approximately 35-40% of total revenue.

ENTERTAINMENT: Tenant plans to host live acoustic music on Friday and 
Saturday evenings (7 PM – 11 PM) and DJ sets for Sunday brunch. 
Tenant will install a small performance area (approximately 60 sq ft) 
in the dining room. Background music system (Sonos) for all other hours.

HOURS OF OPERATION:
  - Mon–Thu: 11:00 AM – 10:00 PM
  - Fri–Sat: 11:00 AM – 1:00 AM
  - Sun: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM (brunch starts 10 AM)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
              TENANT IMPROVEMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Tenant shall, at Tenant's sole cost and expense, perform the following 
improvements to convert the Premises from retail to restaurant use:

KITCHEN:
  - Full commercial kitchen buildout
  - Type I hood ventilation system with makeup air unit
  - Fire suppression system (Ansul wet chemical) in kitchen hood
  - 3-compartment sink installation (18" x 18" basins)
  - Two (2) dedicated handwashing stations
  - Walk-in cooler (8' x 10') and walk-in freezer (6' x 8')
  - Grease interceptor installation (minimum 50 GPM)
  - Sushi bar with refrigerated display case

DINING ROOM:
  - Full bar build (8 bar seats, draft system, 3-bay underbar sink)
  - New flooring: commercial non-slip tile in kitchen, hardwood in dining
  - Performance area with basic sound system and stage lighting
  - Remove carpet throughout (CRITICAL: carpet not permitted in food service)

BUILDING SYSTEMS:
  - Electrical upgrade: 100A panel → 400A service (required for kitchen)
  - Plumbing: new water and waste lines for kitchen and bar
  - HVAC upgrade for kitchen ventilation and dining room comfort
  - Gas line installation for commercial range, fryers, and ramen cookers

RESTROOMS:
  - Convert single retail restroom to two (2) ADA-compliant restrooms
  - Add required handwashing station in restroom hallway

ESTIMATED BUILDOUT COST: $285,000 – $340,000 (per contractor bid)
ESTIMATED BUILDOUT TIMELINE: 12–16 weeks
