PERMITTED USE:
The Premises shall be used solely for the operation of a full-service 
Japanese restaurant with alcoholic beverage service, including but not 
limited to dine-in, takeout, and delivery services. Tenant intends to 
apply for a Type 47 (On-Sale General — Eating Place) liquor license 
from the California Department of Alcoholic Beverage Control.

TENANT IMPROVEMENTS:
Tenant shall, at Tenant's sole cost and expense, perform the following 
improvements to convert the Premises from retail to restaurant use:
- Full commercial kitchen buildout including Type I hood ventilation
- Installation of 3-compartment sink and handwashing stations
- Grease interceptor installation (minimum 50 GPM)
- ADA-compliant restroom renovation (2 restrooms)
- New flooring: commercial non-slip tile in kitchen, hardwood in dining
- Fire suppression system (Ansul) installation in kitchen hood
- HVAC upgrade for kitchen ventilation and makeup air

OUTDOOR DINING:
Landlord consents to Tenant applying for a sidewalk dining permit from 
the City of San Francisco for up to 8 outdoor seats on the Valencia 
Street frontage. Sidewalk width at premises: approximately 14 feet.
Tenant shall be solely responsible for obtaining all necessary permits.

SEATING CAPACITY:
Tenant plans for approximately 45 indoor seats (including 8 bar seats)

to fire department occupancy approval.

SIGNAGE:
